Rice Production in Asia

This section provides an overview of agricultural production trends across Asia. Data covers major rice-producing countries including China, India, Philippines, Vietnam, and Thailand.

Indicators include production volume, yield per hectare, harvested area, irrigated share, consumption, imports, exports, and year-on-year growth.

All values are structured to support time series analysis and cross-country comparison from 2010 to 2025.
